Places to eat in Christmas Eve & Day@NYC

Many of the boroughs has Chinese resturants open for Christmas but this is not your only option.
Check out the holiday festivities at the restaurants on our Places to eat in Christmas Season List. Remember to call ahead for specific hours and menu offerings.
